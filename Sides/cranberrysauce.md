# Cranberry Sauce
## Ingredients
- 1 package Fresh Cranberries
- 1 cup Sugar
- 1 cup Orange Juice
- 1 Tbsp Orange Zest
- 1 Cinnamon Stick

## Method
1. Rinse cranberries and remove damaged berries
2. Combine all ingredients into sauce pan over medium heat
3. Bring to a boil for 10 minutes, stirring every few minutes
4. Remove from heat and remove cinnamon stick
5. Let chill for at least 2 hours
6. Enjoy
