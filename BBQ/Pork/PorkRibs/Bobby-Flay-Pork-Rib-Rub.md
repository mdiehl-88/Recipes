# Bobby Flay's Pork Rib Rub
## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp Cumin
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp chili powder 
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp Chipotle 
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp white pepper

## Method
1. mix all ingredients into a large bowl and stir to mix thoroughly.
