# Piedmont BBQ Sauce
## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cup cider vinegar
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

## Methods
1. Combine ingredents in sauce pain over medium heat.
2. Stir frequently and bring to simmer.
3. Allow to cool and pour into sauce bottle.
